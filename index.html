Welcome to the Infernal Cremation

Totten Korps is a brutal death metal band from Santiago de Chile. Formed in 1989, the group consists of his founder members guitarists Fernando Toro, Francisco Torres and bassist Patricio Garcias, voices Andrés "Sadax" Peña and drummer Ignacio Navarro.
